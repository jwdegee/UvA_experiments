Regarding the conditions for the PPI session.
=============================================

Stimuli:
    - Background: white noise at 70db
    - Probe: 40ms pulse at 120 db
    - Prepulse 1: 20ms pulse at 74 db
    - Prepulse 2: 20ms pulse at 78 db
    - Prepulse 3: 20ms pulse at 82 db

Trial types:
    - No stimulation (maintain background)
    - Probe (40 ms)
    - Prepulse 1 (20 ms) 
    - Prepulse 2 (20 ms) 
    - Prepulse 3 (20 ms) 
    - Prepulse 1 (20 ms) --> Background (80 ms) --> Probe (40 ms)
    - Prepulse 2 (20 ms) --> Background (80 ms) --> Probe (40 ms)
    - Prepulse 3 (20 ms) --> Background (80 ms) --> Probe (40 ms)
